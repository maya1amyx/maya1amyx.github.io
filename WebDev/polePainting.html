Pole Painting

Near the end of the 2018 school year, the club approached school staff to recomend permanent artwork on the campus. These ugly poles were rusting and the old paint was chipped before we added a new layer of primer colors. The club split into groups to design different sections of pols around the school, creating fun imagery and color.
